Byte 4b: Mobile Byte

This is the Mobile Byte (Byte 4b) of the Interactive Data Science class.

Question: Where did Shaleen spend most of his time last week apart from his home?

The story the visualization tells


This is a visualization of the top hotspots in Pittsburgh where Shaleen spent more than 5 minutes between October 10 and October 16 2018. We are trying to evaluate where Shaleen spent most of his time apart from his home in the duration of the study.

As we can see, Shaleen spent most of his time last week at Gates Hillman Center, Carnegie Mellon University.

Like a typical CMU SCS student, the only life Shaleen has is shown by the ocassional trips to Chipotle, Subway and Yuva India Restaurant on Craig Street close to CMU Campus.

Shaleen also wasted a significant amount of time at bus stops, Bellefont and Negley Ave, totalling up to 51 minutes in the last 1 week.

Since the flu season is around the corner, Shaleen also spent a teensy bit of his time getting a flu shot at the CMU Health Services Center at Morewood Avenue.
