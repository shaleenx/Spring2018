Accountability: About This Data Set

The data contains all time F1 driver ranking statistics until 2017. It accounts for the number of races, number of times the driver stood 1st, 2nd and 3rd, number of times they made it to the podium, number of times they started at the pole position, number of times the driver made the fastest lap in the race, and the official total points for the driver.

Where did this data come from?

The data was imported from the F1 Fansite Blog which has compiled this dataset from Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA) sources.

Who has access to it?

The dataset is made available on the internet and is publicly accessible to read. The dataset is editable only by the website administrators and editors.
